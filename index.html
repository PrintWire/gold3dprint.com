Why Precious Metal 3D Printing?

Traditional lost-wax casting limits design to what a mold can release. SLM precious metal printing creates internal voids, undercuts, and lattices that casting cannot achieve.

  • ● Internal voids — reduce weight, preserve visual volume
  • ● Interlocking components in a single print build
  • ● Filigree to 0.1mm wall thickness accuracy
  • ● No casting sprues or investment material waste
  • ● Hallmark-compliant precious metal composition
  • ● Custom alloy compositions available on request
